Current Exhibition

BELONGING(S)

Dora Dalila Cheffi

Date 26 February 2026
Vernissage 7–10 pm
Venue UrArTu Gallery
Warehouse 39, Al Serkhal Avenue, Dubai
Artist Dora Dalila Cheffi

Belonging unfolds across languages, geographies, memories, and invisible ties. Identity is rarely confined to a single place or definition. It is layered, shifting, and continuously evolving; we are never exactly who we were yesterday, and we will surely be different tomorrow.

Inspired by Amin Maalouf's essay In the Name of Identity, the exhibition BE LO NG I N G (S) celebrates the richness — and the privilege — of inhabiting multiple worlds, and the subtle ways these intersections shape who we are becoming. In a time marked by movement and interwoven cultures, belonging becomes less a fixed state than an ongoing dialogue. Identities do not compete; they coexist, nourishing and expanding one another.

At the same time, the exhibition explores the simplicity of moments of celebration: dancing, gatherings around a table, shared meals. In these familiar yet meaningful scenes, belonging emerges — in the warmth of being together, where joy, connection, and togetherness unfold naturally.
