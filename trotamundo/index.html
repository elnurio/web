Rhadoo

A six-hour generative visual performance unfolding alongside Rhadoo's extended set at Trotamundo Festival.

The system was built to evolve continuously, responding to subtle shifts in rhythm, texture, and atmosphere. Rather than following predefined scenes, the visuals operated as a living structure — unfolding, dissolving, and reforming in sync with the progression of the music.

Over the course of the set, the visual language transitioned gradually, maintaining a delicate balance between repetition and transformation.

Micro-changes accumulated into larger movements, mirroring the hypnotic and minimal nature of the sound.

Nicolas Lutz, Binh, Masda

A seven-hour visual continuum accompanying their back-to-back set.
